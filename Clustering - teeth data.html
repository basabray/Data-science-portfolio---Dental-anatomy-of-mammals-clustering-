About The Dataset:¶

  • The dataset has the numbers of different kinds of teeth (for both top and bottom jaws) for a variety of mammals.
  • There is no target/label/dependent column in this dataset to make any prediction.
  • Hence, will apply custering on this dataset, describing the data based on similarities between them.
  • Applied KMeans and Hierarchical alogorithms

Dental Anatomy Of Mammals: Basic information before digging into the data¶

  • Mammals have teeth of different sizes and shapes.
  • Different teeth are present in mammals not just for eating but for different tasks like defense from other animal.
  • These specialized teeth include: Incisors, Canines, Premolars, Molars
  • Due to biological evaluation with time some mammals may have all these kind of teeth present but not using all of these now.
  • And some might have lost any specific kind of teeth due to lack of usability of that particular teeth.
  • So based on such similarity mmamals can be grouped together.

Inferences Based On Cannies teeth: Sometimes the canines are used in holding the prey, mainly seen in carnivorous mammals.¶

  • Cluster1: Cluster1 mammals do not have cannies at all. With time biological changes happen and due to that process some mammals lost their cannies. So, cluster1 might be such animals who do not have cannies teeth now and they do not eat meat. For example : Rabbit, Squirrel

  • Cluster0: Cluster0 mammals have both Top and Bottom cannies.So, cluster0 might be such animals who eats meat. For example : Wolf, Bear, Jaguar. So, they may belong to 'Carnivorous' mammals.

  • Cluster2: Cluster2 mammals who either have Top cannie or Bottom cannie. So they may belong to 'Omnivores' mammals.

Inferences Based On Incisors teeth: The incisors are mainly used for cutting the food material.¶

  • Cluster0: Culter0 are those animals who have the average right top incisors of aprroximately 3. Their function is cutting, slicing, or gnawing food into manageable pieces that fit into the mouth for further chewing. So clearly these are the type of 'carnivorous'.

  • Both Cluster1 and Cluster2 are having the average right top incisors of aprroximately 1

  • So all the types of mammals are having Incisors teeth as for each of them need to cut the food even if it is some plant based food. Just the number of the Incisors teeth differ in them.

Inferences Based On Premolars teeth: The premolars are used for grinding the food materials.¶

  • Cluster0: Culter0 are those animals who have the maximum no of average premolar teeth aprroximately 3.4 to 3.6(both top and bottom premolars).
  • As they eat meat they might need more grinding than other type of mammals. These are used to slice meat and skin. Hence number of grinding teeth is higher in comparison to other 2 clusters. Like - Jaguar
  • Cluster1: These are the mammals with the least no of premolars. So these are the animals who need less crushing or grinding in comparison to the other 2 clusters.Like - Rabbit
  • Cluster2: These are the mammals with average no of premolars approximately as 2.5 to 2.7. So this is not as low as the cluster1 and also not as high as the cluster0. So these might be those animals who needs less grinding than meat eaters but need crushing because they might eat plant based food like grass. Like - Deer, Elk
  • So all the types of mammals are having Premoalrs teeth as for each of them need to grind/crush the food but the only difference is in the number of of theeth in the top and bottom jaws.

Conclusion based on KMeans using 3 clusters:¶

  • It seems that the cluster0 belongs the carnivorus mammals who eat animal meat mostly.
  • It seems that the cluster1 have mammals who eat mostly grass, fruits, seeds, grains like Squirrel, Pike, Rabbit, House Mouse etc. So, we can group them as Herbivores.
  • It seems that the cluster2 have both Omnivores like bats and also few Herbivores like Deer as well. Though there are different kinds of bats present who differs in their food habit.

Conclusion:¶

  • The given data can broadly grouped into 3 different clusters: Carnivores, Harbivores and Omnivores
  • Cluster0: Carnivores who are meat eating animals have cannies teeth in top and bottom both jaws and the rest 2 clusters do not have cannies teeth. And also canovores have high number of Insciors teeth in comparosin to the rest clusters.
  • Cluster1: Harbivores who eats mostly grass, fruits, seeds, grains and that's why they do not have cannies teeth. And they have less number of Insciors teeth.
  • Cluster2: It seems that the cluster2 have both Omnivores like bats and a few Herbivore like Deer as well. Bats and deers are having the same number of premolar and molar teeth and this may be the reason that they are grouped into the same cluster. Though from eating habit they differ but as the number are similar they have been clustered together here.
